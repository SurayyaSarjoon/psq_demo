Built to be the one call you need for pests and cleaning.

Principal Services was started to close a simple gap in the Qatar market: households and businesses were hiring one company for pests and another for cleaning. We trained one team to do both, properly.

Our story

Two crafts, one accountable team

Pest control and cleaning look like separate industries, but on the ground they solve the same problem: a property that doesn't feel fully cared for. A kitchen that isn't properly cleaned attracts pests. A pest problem left untreated undoes even the best clean. So we built Principal Services around both, with technicians cross-trained in inspection, safe treatment, and cleaning standards.

Today we work with homeowners, tenants, offices, hotels, warehouses and food facilities across Doha and the wider Qatar market — from a single one-off treatment to ongoing maintenance contracts.
